Why TLove Enterprises LLC?

TLove Enterprises LLC is a real estate solutions company located in Salem, OR that specializes in buying and selling homes. We’re experts at finding solutions for people who are in the market to buy their next investment property or are looking to sell a home.

Our team has experience with all types of buying and selling scenarios, and we understand how to make every transaction simple and stress-free. We own and sell our homes and are confident that we have something to meet the needs of every seller and buyer. We are certain that we can find a win-win solution for you.



We Are a Real Estate Investing Company


Our company buys and sells homes. We will not be acting as your real estate agent or broker if we buy your house or sell you a house. We are excited about working with you directly OR with your agent or broker if you are currently represented by one. We are focused on improving neighborhoods and redeveloping the areas where we work.
